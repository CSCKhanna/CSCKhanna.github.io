April's CSC Final Project Page

Welcome to my CSC Final Project Page

The program I've created is an interactive game called "Poke a Moji" (inspired by mole mash)
where players poke a bitmoji character within a certain time frame to grow a moji swarm. The
game consists of several scenes: splash screen, game screen, end screen, and a thank you screen.

On the splash screen, players are greeted with a colorful flashing background, shadowed text,
and instructions to start the game. They are also introduced to the main character, my bitmoji.
Clicking the "Start" button transitions the game to the game scene.

In the game screen, the player has 10 seconds to click on the white-shirted bitmoji character
to earn points and grow the swarm of smaller mojis. The background color changes randomly with
each refresh. As the game progresses, the frame rate increases based on the player's score.

When the game time reaches 10 seconds, the game transitions to the end screen. Here, the player
can see their final score and have options to either restart the game, continue growing their
swarm, go back to the home screen, or view a thank you message to the CSC teaching team. The thank
you screen displays the names of team members along with a thank you message. The player can choose
to return to the home screen from here as well.



HOW TO PLAY:

1. Poke the white-shirted bitmoji character within 10 seconds to grow a swarm.

2. The splash screen appears with a flashing background and instructions.
Click the "Start" button to begin the game.

3. On the main game screen you'll see a white-shirted bitmoji character.
Your task is to click (poke) on bitmoji as many times as possible within
the 10-second time limit.

4. To poke the bitmoji, click on it with your mouse. Each successful click
increases the moji swarm size by one.

5. Your current swarm size is displayed in the top left corner of the screen.
Keep an eye on it as you play.

6. A timer at the top of the screen shows you how much time you have left to
poke the bitmoji. Once the timer reaches 10 seconds, the game will end.

7. On the end scree your swarm size will be displayed along with options to restart
the game,continue playing to grow the swarm, go back to the home screen, or view a
thank you message to the CSC teaching team.

8. If you want to play again, simply click the "Restart a New Swarm" button on the end
screen. This will set the swarm size and time to zero.

9. If you want to continue playing with the swarm of mojis, click the "Keep Swarm
Growing" button on the end screen. This will reset your time and add to your current swarm size.

10. To view a thank you message to the CSC teaching team, click the "Thank you CSC Team!"
button on the end screen. You will have the option to restart the game from this screen.



Have fun poking the bitmoji and see how big your moji swarm can be before the time runs out!
